About Our AI Health Chatbot

Our AI-Driven Public Health Chatbot is designed to raise awareness and provide timely information about various diseases. It helps users identify symptoms, learn preventive measures, and access reliable health guidance instantly.

From common illnesses like Dengue and Malaria to critical updates about infectious diseases, our chatbot assists communities in staying informed and making proactive health decisions. It is accessible 24/7, making public health knowledge available anytime.

Patient / Disease Information

Dengue

Symptoms: High fever, severe headache, pain behind the eyes, joint and muscle pain, nausea, vomiting, rash.

Prevention: Use mosquito nets, eliminate standing water, wear long-sleeved clothing, use mosquito repellents.

Treatment: No specific medicine; supportive care like hydration, rest, and fever management is recommended. Severe cases may need hospitalization.

Additional Info: Dengue is caused by the dengue virus transmitted by Aedes mosquitoes. Early detection is crucial to avoid complications.

Malaria

Symptoms: Fever, chills, sweating, headache, nausea, fatigue, body aches.

Prevention: Mosquito control, use of insecticide-treated bed nets, antimalarial prophylaxis when traveling to endemic areas, wearing protective clothing.

Treatment: Antimalarial medications like chloroquine, artemisinin-based combination therapy (ACT), depending on type and region.

Additional Info: Caused by Plasmodium parasites, transmitted by Anopheles mosquitoes. Prompt treatment prevents severe complications.

COVID-19

Symptoms: Fever, cough, sore throat, shortness of breath, loss of smell/taste, fatigue, body aches.

Prevention: Vaccination, mask-wearing, social distancing, hand hygiene, avoiding crowded places.

Treatment: Mild cases: rest, hydration, and over-the-counter medication. Severe cases: hospitalization, oxygen therapy, antivirals, or other supportive care.

Additional Info: Caused by SARS-CoV-2 virus. Early detection, isolation, and vaccination are key to controlling spread.
